What is BeatUp?

BeatUp is a music streaming platform that allows users to listen to, download, and share their favorite music tracks from various genres and artists.

How can I create an account on BeatUp?

You can create an account on BeatUp by clicking the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and filling out the registration form with your details.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial for new users to explore our premium features before committing to a subscription.

Can I download music for offline listening?

Yes, premium subscribers can download music tracks for offline listening on their devices.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time by going to your account settings and selecting the "Cancel Subscription" option.
